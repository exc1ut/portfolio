Dmozzi

The Process

Dmozzi is a convenient and fast food delivery service from restaurants. Order on the website or in the mobile application, pay by card or cash, and we will deliver your favorite food quickly right to your door.

Choosing the best restaurants, we cover all the dishes that you might like: juicy burgers or steaks, fragrant pizza or pasta, wok or rolls, porridge for breakfast or pilaf for lunch - Dmozzi brings together all the cuisines available in the city so that you can time to try something new.
